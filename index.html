About David

Real Change Starts Here

As a military veteran and licensed psychotherapist, I bring a practical, grounded perspective to therapy. I've spent years working in environments where pressure is high and people are expected to carry a lot on their own. That experience shaped how I approach helping others work through difficult moments in their lives.

I work with adults who feel stuck, overwhelmed, or worn down by anxiety, depression, trauma, or major life stress. Many of the people I see are used to pushing through things on their own, until eventually it becomes too heavy to carry alone.

My approach to therapy is direct, practical, and focused on real progress. Together we identify what's keeping you stuck, develop tools that actually work in everyday life, and move forward at a pace that makes sense for you.

If you're ready for something to change, this is a place where that work can begin.

Currently accepting clients ages 18 and up.

Trauma & PTSD Therapy

Trauma changes how you see the world, your body, and yourself. It might show up as hypervigilance, nightmares, emotional numbness, or feeling disconnected from the people you love. These aren't signs of weakness — they're your nervous system's response to overwhelming experiences.

As a military veteran, I bring a depth of understanding to trauma work that goes beyond clinical training. I know what it means to carry something heavy and not know how to put it down. And I know that healing is real — because I've seen it, and lived it.

PTSD

Flashbacks, hypervigilance, avoidance — we address the root, not just the symptoms.

Trauma-Informed Care

Every step of our work is guided by a deep understanding of how trauma lives in the body and mind.

Emotional Trauma

Grief & Loss
