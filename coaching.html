Coaching vs. Therapy

What Is Therapeutic Coaching?

And how can it help you?

Coaching enables me to work with clients from anywhere in the world, offering practical, high-impact techniques to help you navigate life's challenges. As a trained therapist, I bring formal training and real-world experience to coaching in a way that is more flexible than traditional therapy allows.


Whether you are a professional feeling stuck, a veteran navigating the transition to civilian life, a couple working through challenges together, or someone facing a major life change — coaching gives you a structured, supportive space to get clarity, build momentum, and move forward.

Who It's For

Does Any of This Sound Like You?

You don't have to be falling apart to benefit from coaching. Most people who reach out are high-functioning, driven individuals who feel like something is off — and can't quite put their finger on why.

💼

The Burned-Out Professional

You have the career, the income, the responsibilities — but you feel empty, exhausted, and like you're just going through the motions. You know you're capable of more. You just can't find your way back to it.

🎖️

The Transitioning Veteran

You served with purpose and structure. Now civilian life feels scattered, directionless, or just plain hard to navigate. You're looking for someone who actually understands what that transition feels like.

💑

The Couple at a Crossroads

You love each other but you keep having the same arguments, the same distance, the same disconnect. Coaching gives you both a structured, honest space to finally hear each other and move forward together.

🔄

The Person in Transition

Divorce. Career change. Loss. A new chapter you didn't plan for. Life has shifted and you're trying to find solid ground again. Coaching helps you stop reacting and start rebuilding — with intention.

"You don't need to have it all figured out before reaching out. You just need to be ready for something to change."

— David Robles, LMSW
