Coaching vs. Therapy

What Is Therapeutic Coaching?

And how can it help you?

Coaching enables me to work with clients from anywhere in the world, offering practical, high-impact techniques to help you navigate life's challenges. As a trained therapist, I bring formal training and real-world experience to coaching in a way that is more flexible than traditional therapy allows.


Whether you are a professional feeling stuck, a veteran navigating the transition to civilian life, a couple working through challenges together, or someone facing a major life change — coaching gives you a structured, supportive space to get clarity, build momentum, and move forward.

Who It's For

Is Coaching Right for You?

You might not be in crisis. You might just feel stuck — like you know something needs to change but you're not sure where to start. Coaching is for people who are ready to take action.

  • → Professionals feeling burned out or unfulfilled
  • → Veterans transitioning to civilian life
  • → Couples working through communication and connection
  • → Anyone navigating a major life transition
  • → People ready for real change but unsure where to start

The Process

How It Works

A simple, focused process designed to get you moving.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a free conversation to understand where you are, what's holding you back, and whether coaching is the right fit.

02

Build Your Plan

Together we identify your goals, remove the obstacles, and map out a clear, actionable path forward.

03

Take Action

Session by session, you build real momentum — with accountability, tools, and support every step of the way.
